Slice of Heaven Italian Restaurant and Pizza

Calgary
Est. 2018

Slice of Heaven is a new, authentic Italian restaurant and pizzeria in downtown Calgary, with a focus on using locally-sourced ingredients to produce our meals. We offer handcrafted pizzas and housemade pasta in a casual, affordable environment. Stop in for breakfast, lunch or dinner and grab a slice of our delicious pizza and watch our master pizza makers toss dough!
